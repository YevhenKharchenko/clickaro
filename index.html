How It Works

  • Match three or more items to spawn heroes.

  • Build and upgrade heroes to enhance their abilities.

  • Use strategy to activate power-ups and boost your defense.

  • Progress through episodes and unlock new challenges.

Heroes

  • You'll unlock different heroes as you play, and they've all got their own special abilities.

  • Unlock and level them up as you match items.

  • Combine hero skills strategically to overcome enemy waves.

  • Heroes grow stronger with each success and support your castle defense.

Power-Ups & Boosts

There are some neat power-ups that can really change how you approach each puzzle. Use boosters to clear tricky tiles or enhance combos. These boosters don't just make things easier - they actually make the game more strategic.

Locations & Valleys

Each new area you unlock has its own look and feel, plus different types of challenges to tackle. You'll run into some weird enemies and find hidden stuff as you work your way through the levels.
